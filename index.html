How WojakRumble Works

A revolutionary platform combining meme culture, artistic competition, and blockchain technology.

1

Admission & Verification

Artists pay an admission fee in $WOR tokens to join an ArtRumble competition, creating immediate value for the coin.

They submit a video proving they created their artwork from start to finish, ensuring authenticity and deterring plagiarism.

Admission fees are pooled into a prize fund, with 10-20% going back to the ArtRumble treasury to sustain the ecosystem.

2

Live-Streamed Art Battles

At the end of each month, WojakRumble competitions are broadcast globally on YouTube and Twitch, transforming art creation into a spectator sport.

Viewers watch artists work in real time, whether they're creating memes, painting, sculpting, murals, coding digital art, or crafting installations.

During the stream, viewers can directly support their favorite artists by tipping them in $WOR or SOL tokens.

3

Voting Power to the People

The winner isn't decided by a panel of elitesâ€”it's chosen by the community.

  • YouTube viewers can vote via likes, super chats, polls, or tips
  • $WOR token holders get additional voting power based on their holdings

This hybrid system ensures fairness while rewarding both engagement and loyalty.

What Happens to Winning Artwork?

Prize Money

Winners receive prize money from the pooled admission fees, tips, and sponsorships.

Giving Back: Fueling Creativity and Charity

WojakRumble is committed to supporting both artists and social impact.

  • A percentage of all revenue (from admission fees, merch sales, and tips) is automatically allocated to WojakRumble's charity fund, which partners with organizations promoting arts education in underserved communities.
